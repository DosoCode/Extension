2. Creating a YouTube Account

To access most features, you'll need a Google account. Visit YouTube.com, click "Sign In" in the top-right, and use or create a Google account. Once logged in, you can customize your profile, subscribe to channels, and start uploading videos.

3. Navigating the YouTube Interface

The homepage features recommended videos based on your history, trending content, and subscriptions. The top search bar lets you find videos, channels, or topics. The left-hand menu includes links to:

  • Subscriptions
  • Library

  • Liked Videos

5. Subscribing to Channels

Click "Subscribe" to follow a channel and get updates. Hit the bell icon next to it to set notifications to "All," "Personalized," or "None."

6. YouTube Shorts

YouTube Shorts are vertical videos under 60 seconds. They are accessible via the "Shorts" tab and can be created directly from the mobile app. Shorts are optimized for mobile viewing and discovery.

8. YouTube Studio Overview

YouTube Studio is the content management dashboard for creators. Key features:

  • Comments: View and respond to viewer comments
  • Monetization: Check eligibility and earnings
  • Customization: Edit channel layout, branding, and basic info
