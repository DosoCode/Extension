Getting Started

  • Visit https://www.amazon.com in your browser.
  • If you don't already have an account, click “Sign in” at the top right and then “Create your Amazon account.”
  • Enter your name, email, and create a secure password.
  • After signing in, you'll have access to all Amazon shopping features.

The Amazon Homepage

The Amazon homepage shows categories, today's deals, and personalized recommendations. At the top, you'll see:

  • Search bar: Type in what you're looking for.
  • Account & Lists: Access your profile, orders, and wish lists.
  • Cart icon: Shows items you're planning to buy.

Searching for Products

Use the search bar at the top. Type in keywords (like “wireless headphones”) and press Enter. You can filter results by brand, price, customer rating, or shipping speed on the left side of the results page.

Understanding Product Pages

A product page includes:

  • Product Title: The name of the item.
  • Price: The cost, sometimes with discounts shown.
  • Delivery Information: Estimated arrival dates and Prime eligibility.
  • Customer Reviews: Star ratings and feedback from other buyers.
  • Add to Cart / Buy Now: Buttons to purchase.

Adding to Cart & Checkout

To buy something, click “Add to Cart.” When you're ready, click the cart icon at the top right and then “Proceed to Checkout.” Enter your shipping address, choose a payment method, and confirm your order.

Managing Orders

To track your packages or view past purchases, hover over “Account & Lists” and select “Your Orders.” You can see delivery updates or request help if something goes wrong.

Returns & Refunds

If you need to return an item, go to “Your Orders,” find the product, and click “Return or replace items.” Amazon will guide you through printing a return label or arranging a pickup.

Account Settings

Under “Account & Lists,” you can manage your login info, payment methods, addresses, and Prime membership. You can also set up one-click purchases or update communication preferences.

Helpful Tips

  • Look for “Prime” items for free and fast shipping.
  • Check “Today's Deals” for discounts on popular items.
  • Add items to your Wish List to save them for later.
  • Use filters (on the left of search results) to narrow choices quickly.
  • Always check customer reviews before buying.

Extra Stuff

  • Subscribe & Save: Save money by setting up recurring deliveries for items like household goods.
  • Amazon Prime: Membership that includes free shipping, Prime Video, and exclusive deals.
  • Gift Cards: Buy and send digital or physical gift cards to others.
  • Amazon Fresh & Grocery: Order groceries online and schedule deliveries.
  • Alexa Shopping: If you have an Echo device, you can shop hands-free using voice commands.
