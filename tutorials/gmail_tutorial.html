Getting Started

  • To begin Using Gmail, you first need a google account. you can visit https://mail.google.com. After you arrive at the site, you will be redirected to a sign-in page. First click 'create account' button in the bottom left.Then chose if you are creating it for your own personal use, for your child or for work.
  • Fill out the form with your name, birthday, gender.
  • Now you will be asked to choose an email address. This email address will be used whenever you want to sign in to your account for gmail or any other google service.
  • Next you need to create a password. This password is needed to sign in to your gmail account and SHOULD NOT be public information. Pick a password you will remember and nobody else would be able to guess.

The Gmail Interface

Once you're signed in, the Gmail interface will display your inbox in the center, a left-hand sidebar, and a toolbar at the top. The inbox is where all incoming and read messages are stored. The sidebar gives access to other folders like Sent, Drafts, Spam, Trash, and any custom labels you’ve created. At the top, you’ll find the search bar, which lets you look up emails quickly, and the Compose button, which opens a new window to write an email. You’ll also find a gear icon for settings and layout options. Gmail often organizes emails into tabs such as Primary (important or personal messages), Social (updates from social networks), and Promotions (marketing emails). You can customize which tabs are visible.

How to write an Email

In the top-left corner of the website, you will see a button that says 'compose'. Press this buton to bring up a 'New Message' box on your screen. This is your email draft. The first line from the top has 'To' written on it. This is where you input who you are sending the email to. You will need their email address. If you dont send an email it will be stored in your Drafts until you've deleted it.

How to attach files

Click the paperclip icon at the bottom of the compose window. Choose a file from your device. Wait for it to upload, then click Send.

Replying to emails

Open the email you want to respond to. CLick the reply button if you want to reply to the sender of the email. and click forward if you want to send the email to someone else.

Settings and Customization

Gmail offers a wide range of customization options. Click the gear icon in the top right and select “See all settings” to adjust general preferences like your email signature, vacation responder, conversation view, and keyboard shortcuts. To personalize your inbox appearance, go to Settings > Theme and select a background image or upload your own.

Security and Privacy

To protect your Gmail account, it's important to keep your password secure and update it regularly. Go to your Google Account's Security section to change your password or update recovery options. For extra protection, enable 2-Step Verification, which requires a second form of authentication (like a code sent to your phone) whenever you sign in from a new device.
