YouTube Guided Tutorial

This section is a simple walkthrough of how to use YouTube, designed for people with little to no technical background. We'll guide you through the basics and show how to get the most out of watching and managing videos.

Getting Started

  • Go to https://www.youtube.com in your web browser.
  • You do not need an account to watch videos, but creating one allows you to subscribe, like, comment, and more.
  • To create an account, click "Sign In" in the top-right corner and follow the prompts to create a Google account.
  • Once logged in, you can customize your YouTube experience.

The YouTube Interface

The YouTube homepage shows a mix of recommended videos, trending content, and channels you may like. The left menu lets you access Home, Shorts, Subscriptions, Library, and History. The top bar includes a search box, upload options, and notifications.

How to Search for Videos

Use the search bar at the top of the screen to find videos. Type what you're looking for (like "pasta recipe" or "beginner yoga") and press Enter. YouTube will show results based on relevance and popularity.

Watching a Video

Click any video to watch it. It will open in a player window. Use the Play, Pause, Volume, and Fullscreen buttons at the bottom. You can also adjust playback speed using the gear icon.

Subscribing to Channels

If you like a creator's videos, click the "Subscribe" button below the video or on their channel page. This helps you see their future uploads in your Subscriptions feed.

Using and Creating Playlists

A playlist is a collection of videos. You can create your own or watch ones others have made. Click "Save" under a video and choose a playlist, or create a new one. Access your playlists from the left-hand menu under "Library."

Captions and Video Quality

Many videos have automatic captions. Click the "CC" icon on the video player to turn them on or off. To change quality, click the gear icon and choose a higher or lower resolution depending on your internet speed.

Managing Your Watch History

YouTube keeps track of videos you've watched. Click "History" in the left menu to revisit past videos. You can remove individual videos or clear your full history. This helps with privacy and keeps your recommendations relevant.

Privacy and Safety Settings

Go to Google Account Settings to control privacy options. You can choose what activity is saved, turn off personalized ads, or enable Restricted Mode to block potentially mature content.

Extra Tips

  • Like and Dislike: Use thumbs up or down to give feedback. It helps YouTube suggest better videos for you.
  • Comments: Scroll down below a video to leave a comment or read what others are saying.
  • Watch Later: Click the clock icon on a video to add it to your "Watch Later" playlist.
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Press K to pause/play, J to rewind 10 seconds, and L to fast forward 10 seconds.
  • YouTube App: You can watch YouTube on phones, tablets, and smart TVs by downloading the app and signing in.
  • Dark Theme: Go to your profile picture > "Appearance" to enable Dark Theme for easier viewing at night.
  • Notifications: After subscribing, click the bell icon to get alerts for new videos from that channel.
